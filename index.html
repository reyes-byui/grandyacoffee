Our Best Sellers

Grandya Coffee's best sellers are customer favorites, known for their refreshing taste and quality ingredients. Try our signature Iced Latte, the vibrant Iced Matcha Latte, or the unique Passion Frappe for a delightful coffee experience.

Premium Coffee

Grandya Coffee uses premium beans: a balanced blend of 50% Arabica and 50% Robusta, medium roasted for optimal flavor. Sourced directly from Mondulkiri, these beans deliver a rich and authentic coffee experience.
