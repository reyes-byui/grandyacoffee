FAQ & Help Center

Effective Date: June 19, 2025

Welcome to the Grandya Coffee FAQ & Help Center! Here you'll find answers to common questions and helpful information about our coffee shop, products, and services.

  1. What are your opening hours?
    We are open daily from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Sunday.
  2. Where are you located?
    You can find us at Wat Damnak, Siem Reap. See the 'Find Us' section for a map and directions.
  3. Do you offer delivery or takeout?
    Yes, we offer both delivery and takeout. Please contact us or order through our website or partner apps.
  4. Can I book a table or reserve for events?
    Yes, you can make reservations for tables or private events. Please use our inquiry form or call us directly.
  5. Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?
    Absolutely! We offer a variety of vegan and gluten-free food and drink options. Ask our staff for details.
  6. How can I contact you?
    Visit our Contact page for phone, email, and a contact form.
  7. How do I provide feedback or suggestions?
    We welcome your feedback! Please use our contact form or speak to a staff member in-store.
