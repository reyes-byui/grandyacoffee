Community Guidelines

Effective Date: June 15, 2025

Welcome to Grandya Coffee! We strive to create a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone—both in our coffee shop and online community. Please follow these guidelines to help us maintain a positive space:

  1. Be Respectful
    Treat all customers, staff, and community members with kindness and respect. Discrimination, harassment, or hate speech will not be tolerated.
  2. Keep It Friendly
    Use polite language and maintain a friendly tone in all interactions, whether in person or online.
  3. Share Constructively
    Feedback and suggestions are welcome, but please keep them constructive and courteous.
  4. Protect Privacy
    Do not share anyone’s personal information without their consent.
  5. No Spam or Promotions
    Avoid posting spam, advertisements, or unrelated promotions in our community spaces.
  6. Follow the Law
    Comply with all applicable laws and regulations when participating in our community.
  7. Report Issues
    If you witness or experience inappropriate behavior, please report it to our staff or through our contact form.

Thank you for helping us make Grandya Coffee a great place for everyone!
